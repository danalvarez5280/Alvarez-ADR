Federico C. Alvarez is a former Denver District Court Judge who offers alternate dispute resolution services as an arbitrator and mediator as an alternative to litigation or to expedite pending litigation. Federico also has experience as a court appointed master in civil and family law cases. Federico has over 30 years of well-balanced experience in litigation and alternate dispute resolution. Federico draws on this breadth of experience to provide efficient and effective services as an arbitrator and mediator. Federico has practiced law in Colorado since 1980 when he graduated from Harvard Law School. Federico also adds his Spanish speaking knowledge to his Alternate Dispute Resolution services.
