ALVAREZ ADR, LLC

Federico C. Alvarez is a current Denver District Court Senior Judge who offers alternate dispute resolution services as an arbitrator and mediator as an alternative to litigation or to expedite pending litigation. Federico also has experience as a court appointed master in civil and family law cases. Federico has 29 years of well-balanced experience in litigation. Federico draws on this breadth of experience to provide efficient and effective services as an arbitrator, mediator and court appointed master. Federico has practiced law in Colorado since 1980 when he graduated from Harvard Law School.

Federico has now affiliated with Dispute Management, Inc. to offer his alternate dispute resolution services along with Daniel C. Himelspach, former Denver District Judge Leslie M. Lawson, and Janis Y. Cella. Dispute Management, Inc. provides mediation, arbitration, court-appointed master and employment fact finding services in all civil, commercial, employment and family law cases. Federico also adds his Spanish speaking knowledge to Dispute Management, Inc.
